Since September 2015, I work as an associate professor (Maître de conférences) at INSA Centre Val de Loire (Blois, France). I do my research within the Laboratoire Gabriel Lamé (LaMé). Previously, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher with Prof. Bruno Cochelin and Prof. Sergio Bellizzi at Centrale Méditérannée and the Laboratoire de Mécanique et d'Acoustique (LMA, Marseille, France). I hold a PhD from Le Mans University, supervised by Dr. André Almeida, Prof. Christophe Vergez and Prof. Bruno Gazengel. I am accredited to supervise research (HDR) by the University of Tours since February 2024.
