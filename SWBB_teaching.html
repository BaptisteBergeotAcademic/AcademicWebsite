Teaching

Since starting my PhD at the University of Le Mans, I have had the opportunity to contribute to the education of young scientists and engineers. My teaching primarily covers mechanics, acoustics and vibrations at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. The main courses I have taught are listed below, along with available teaching materials. A more detailed description, including some earlier courses, can be found in my Habilitation (HDR) manuscript under the “Personal Record” (Notice personnelle) section.
